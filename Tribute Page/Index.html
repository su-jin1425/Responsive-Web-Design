Albert Einstein

Theoretical Physicist who revolutionized science with his theories of relativity and quantum mechanics.

Here's a timeline of Albert Einstein's life:

  • 1879 - Born in Ulm, Germany
  • 1889 - Introduced to scientific and philosophical writings by medical student Max Talmud.
  • 1905 - Published papers on quantum theory and special theory of relativity in Annalen der Physik.
  • 1912 - Appointed professor of theoretical physics at Zurich Polytechnic.
  • 1914 - Formulated the general theory of relativity.
  • 1922 - Awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics for his explanation of the photoelectric effect.
  • 1955 - Passed away in Princeton, New Jersey.
